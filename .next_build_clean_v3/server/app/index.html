Meet Dr. Rohit VermaB.P.T, M.P.T (Neuro Rehabilitation & Musculoskeletal Recovery)

Dr. Rohit Verma

7+ Years Clinical Experience

Dr. Rohit Verma is Noida's leading physical therapist specializing in comprehensive Neurological & Orthopaedic Rehabilitation. Over a career spanning more than 7 years, he has successfully guided over 2,100 patients back to peak mobility and pain-free living.

Having worked across renowned multi-specialty hospitals and active sports academies, Dr. Rohit Verma recognized that recovery is fastest, safest, and most lasting when clinical expertise is combined with a stress-free environment. This realization drove him to pioneer the PhysioVenture Home Rehabilitation Program in Noida.

He combines cutting-edge physical therapeutic methods (like neuro-developmental therapy, chiropractic spine adjustments, dry needling, and clinical myofascial release) with empathetic, personalized care, ensuring every treatment plan aligns with the patient's lifestyle goals.
